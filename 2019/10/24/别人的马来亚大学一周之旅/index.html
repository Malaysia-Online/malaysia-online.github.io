    • 别人的马来亚大学一周之旅和曾经的学校经历
    发表于 2019-10-24| 更新于 2019-11-28|马来西亚留学移民
    阅读量:

     

    FeiFei

    序

    当我8岁的时候，妈妈教育我，“在学校里面，要交一些朋友，不然的话，你会被其他人冷落的“。于是，我对一个男生说想跟他交朋友，并且我还送他一个新的笔记本和一支铅笔，但他直接拒绝了我。小学毕业以后，我还是不懂，跟男生在一块儿如何克服害羞。

    当我12岁的时候，爸爸告诉我，“一定要找一个榜样学习，不然，你对学习会不知所措”。于是，我跟班长一起加入了课后羽球队，并且每天跟他讨论课后作业。因此，在班上我一直是尖子生，成绩保持得很好。

    当我16岁的时候，爸妈跟我说，“要跟你的同学竞争，不然你如何进入理想的大学呢”。于是，我上课认真听讲，可是我的学业还是遇到一些难题。我的同桌是一位很友善的女生，她鼓励我，因此我没有放弃。而且，从那时候开始，我对女生同胞开始充满深深的敬意。

    当我20岁的时候，爸妈没有叮嘱我任何事情。在大学里，我认识了一些朋友，并加入了一些俱乐部。我玩耍、阅读、寻找自我，并且找到了自己的目标。

    要不了多久，我就会30岁，我的爸妈也会老去。我依然在世界某个地方漫游，期待成为更好的自己。虽然我不清楚哪里是我的终点，但我会继续开启像现在这样的旅程！

    Once I was eight years old, my mommy told me “go and make some friends in your primary school or you’ll be isolated by others”. So I asked a boy to be my friend and brought him a new notebook and pencil, but he rejected me. After I finished my courses in primary school, I still didn’t know how to overcome shyness with boys.

    Once I was twelve years old, my dad told me “go and find a model partner or you’ll be lost in your study”. Then I joined post-class badminton team with our class monitor, and discussed my homework with him every day so, I was always among the top few students with the best academic records.

    Once I was sixteen years old, my parents told me “go and compete with your schoolmates or you’ll never enter your dream school “. Then I listened attentively to lectures from teachers, but I still faced problems getting into studies. My deskmate, a very kind girl, encouraged me, so I refused to give it up. From then on, I started to have a deep respect for we girls.

    Once I was twenty years old, I was not been told anything by my parents. I made some friends and joined various clubs in my college. I play, I read, I find myself, and I see my goal.

    Soon I’ll be thirty years old, my parents will be getting old. And I am still roaming the world in hopes of becoming a better me. I don’t know where is my destination, but I keep on taking a journey like this.
